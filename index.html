having been born in hong kong and raised in new york, i find myself "too chinese" for americans and "too westernized" for the east. i am part of these two worlds, yet feel like i belong to neither. after traveling and experiencing different cultures, i realized that i should not limit myself to just one place and one culture; instead i should strive to become a global citizen.

we are all visitors to this time, this place. we are just passing through. our purpose here is to observe, to learn, to grow, to love... and then we return home.

this is an australian aboriginal proverb that i try to live by. i believe that all living beings are equal. we should spread love and help each other grow to become the best versions of ourselves. "the universe is not a place of separateness, it is a place of entanglement. we are connected to others in ways we cannot fathom." when it is our time, we shall leave this world without taking more than we have given.
